"And There Was One" is a clue like game where you and your friends are detectives collecting clues to figure out how the person was killed. Can you figure out the weapon, person and location?

Murder Files is a game of mafia, each player is assigned a role and all must work together to find out who the killer is. Work together or decieve one another, one must win.

Brain Invaders is a party like game where you and your friends are put into a party like simulation, you can win by being the last alive or defeating the boss by completing mini games including, challenges, trivia, and charades.
